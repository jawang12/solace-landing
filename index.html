Our Motivation Behind Solace

Studies have shown that writing or speaking whatâ€™s on our minds is extremely beneficial to our sense of self and well-being. Unfortunately many people don't take the time out to write out what's on their minds and others are often embarrassed or unwilling to tell their problems to another person. This is the problem we are trying to solve.

With Solace we created an easier and more accurate method of self-introspection. Watson's response is sent back as percentiles so it compares users to the millions of responses it has received, allowing for the most accurate analysis. We take this analysis and create visual representations, allowing users to learn and discover more about themselves.
